Content

Home page

What is Haloterminal? The site is for dedicated halo fans who want to stay in touch with the community especially what the community is creating. The site also links to socials in the about section below. If you want to improve your experience with the community with this game then this is the site for you. What can I do on this Site? This site helps getting fans to be in the know on community creations that come from the game especially the in game forge mode. To find limitless forge creations that you can play nonstop, check out the Forge Creations tab for more information. The site will also help them to know all of the latest news from the series, including leaks, trailers, and comments on social media. Click on the News tab to be in the know now!

Images for the Home page

Forge Creations

Why is Forge so Important? This Page is all about the community and what they create using the Forge tools. Forge is a massive level creator given to players to create a variety of levels, games, modes, and more using the in-game sandbox. Forge has recently been given massive upgrades that allows fans to create so much more with the tools available. The best creations are featured here, updated every day. The official game site doesn't have a dedicated section for displaying and showcasing many creations from the community, so this site is mainly dedicated to that feature. What can I find in the Forge Creations Section? You will be able to search for specific games and modes that you're looking to play. Want to play campaign style maps using the new AI toolkit? Maybe you want to see classic maps remade in Halo Infinite? Or maybe you want to have fun in wacky "action-sack" style modes and maps. The section will showcase categories of forge creations to select and look into further, showing pictures for each category.

News

What can I find on the News tab? This page includes recent news stories and major leaks that have to do with Halo Infinite, and the Halo series in general. The site will feature the latest trailers straight from the company behind the game, to comments by developers on social media, major events in the community, and even down to leaks and speculation. This is the place to be updated on a daily and weekly basis and never miss a beat.
